AVAILABLE COLOR OPTIONS

The BMW G 310 R is available in three exciting colour variants: Kyanite Blue Metallic (Style Passion), Cosmic Black 2 and Limestone Metallic (Style Sport)

SINGLE-CYLINDER FOUR-STROKE ENGINE

313 cc Water Cooled Single-Cylinder 4-Stroke Engine with an output of 34hp at 9,500 rpm and a maximum torque of 28Nm at 7,500 rpm.

BMW G310R - SPECIAL RED FRAME

The new BMW G310R Features a Style Sport Colour scheme – a special limestone metallic shade has been combined with red rims, and a red frame to create a bold, sporty look that truly leaves an impression.

FOUR STAGE LEVER FOR BRAKE AND CLUTCH

Both the clutch and the handbrake lever are now adjustable with four settings -providing unparalleled ergonomic benefit and making it suitable for every kind of rider.

RIDE-BY-WIRE TECHNOLOGY WITH E-THROTTLE

The new Ride-by-Wire system transmits the riders commands directly via a sensor on the twist grip to the engine control system, resulting in even finer control and improved throttle response.

NEW LED HEADLIGHT AND LED FLASHING TURN INDICATORS

The new BMW G310R now come with full-LED Headlights as standard for improved night visibility. The bike also features flashing LED turn indictors for increased visibility in traffic.
